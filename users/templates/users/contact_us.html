Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get support for my account?

For account-related issues, please reach out to us via email at info@jmsadvisory.in with your account details, and our support team will assist you as soon as possible.

What is your response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST). Complex issues may require additional time.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation?

Absolutely! Please send us a message using the contact form above mentioning your preferred date and time, and we'll arrange a convenient time for you.

Is there a phone support option?

Currently, we offer email support, but you can call us during business hours at +91 98765 43210. We also welcome detailed emails with your inquiry.

How do I report a technical issue?

Please describe the issue in detail when contacting us:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened (error message if any)
  • Your browser and operating system
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
How do I provide feedback or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear your suggestions! Send us an email with your feedback or feature request at info@jmsadvisory.in. Your input helps us improve SellSharp.
