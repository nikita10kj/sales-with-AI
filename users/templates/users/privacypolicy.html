Privacy Policy

At SellSharp (https://sellsharp.co/), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website to generate personalized sales emails.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Information: When you use our service, you may enter your name, email address, company details, service/product details, and information about your target audience.
  • Usage Data: We collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, access times, and pages viewed.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze site usage.

Note: We do not collect payment or financial information, as our website does not offer payment processing.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized sales emails based on the details you provide
  • Communicate with you regarding your use of the service or respond to inquiries
  • Improve and optimize our website and services
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • If you sign in with your Google/Microsoft account, we use your mail send access (with your explicit consent) to deliver personalized emails generated via our platform. This access is limited to sending emails only and does not involve reading or storing your email content.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: For analytics and website functionality (e.g., Google Analytics) /li>
  • Legal Requirements:  If required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to personalize your experience and analyze traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact us at info@jmsadvisory.in to exercise your rights.

6. Data Storage and Security

We store user data securely using Microsoft Azure services. All user-related data, including names, email addresses, and generated sales content, is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database hosted on Azure.

We take data protection seriously and implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and encrypted at rest using Azure-managed encryption.
  • Access Control: Database and application access is restricted using role-based access controls (RBAC) and secure authentication protocols.
  • Backups & Reliability: Regular automated backups are maintained to ensure data availability and recovery in case of unexpected issues.
  • Infrastructure Security: Azure provides built-in security and compliance features that help protect data from unauthorized access, data loss, or malicious activity.

We do not store users’ Google/Microsoft account credentials. OAuth access tokens are stored securely and used strictly for the intended scope (e.g., sending emails), and can be revoked at any time by the user.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.
